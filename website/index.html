Our Approach

Not another symptom checker.

Most health apps give you generic answers with no sources and forget you exist after each session. We built something fundamentally different.

Citation-backed, always

Every medical claim in every response includes a numbered citation linking to its source. If we can't cite it, we don't say it.

Learns and follows up

The app builds a private health profile from your conversations and keeps learning. It remembers what you're dealing with and can follow up on unresolved symptoms, medication changes, and ongoing conditions.

Emergency detection

If your symptoms suggest a medical emergency like chest pain, stroke signs, or severe breathing difficulty, the app immediately tells you to call 911.

Private by design

Your health data is never sold or shared for advertising. Conversations and health profiles stay between you and the app. Period.
